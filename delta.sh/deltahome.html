About Chennai

In Chennai, areas with heavy rainfall, particularly coastal and low-lying zones, are more prone to water logging incidents. Coastal regions like Mylapore and Besant Nagar face increased risk due to their proximity to the sea and susceptibility to storm surges. Adyar and Velachery, situated in the southern part of the city, often experience water logging during intense rainfall events. Urbanization and inadequate drainage exacerbate the problem, with infrastructure unable to cope with the volume of water. Understanding these localized risks is essential for mitigating water logging impacts and enhancing urban resilience in Chennai.
